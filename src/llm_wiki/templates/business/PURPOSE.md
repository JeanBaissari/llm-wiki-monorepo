# Business — Domain Purpose

This template governs wikis for organizational operations, strategic planning, and stakeholder management. It is designed for founders, managers, executives, and operations professionals who need a structured knowledge base of meetings, decisions, projects, and stakeholder relationships.

## What this domain covers

- **Meetings** — structured records with dates, attendees, and action items
- **Decisions** — documented rationale, alternatives considered, and outcomes
- **Projects** — scope, status, milestones, risks, and deliverables
- **Stakeholders** — profiles of individuals, groups, or organizations with vested interests

## When to use this template

Use when your wiki serves as an organizational memory — tracking what was decided, who was involved, and what needs to happen next.

## Relationship to base types

The base entity/concept/source types still apply:
- **Entities** — companies, departments, vendors, partners
- **Concepts** — OKRs, SWOT analysis, RACI matrix, agile methodology
- **Sources** — industry reports, market research, internal documents
