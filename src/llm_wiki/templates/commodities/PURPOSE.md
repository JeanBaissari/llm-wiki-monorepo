# Commodities — Domain Purpose

This template governs wikis for tracking and analyzing physical commodity markets. It is designed for traders, analysts, supply-chain professionals, and researchers who need a structured, queryable knowledge base of commodities data.

## What this domain covers

- **Commodity spot/futures fundamentals** — production, consumption, inventories, pricing
- **Market reports** — daily/weekly analytics, technical and fundamental summaries
- **Cross-commodity correlations** — spreads, ratios, hedge ratios, macro factor links
- **Supply-chain intelligence** — logistics routes, bottleneck analysis, geopolitical risk

## When to use this template

Use when your wiki's primary focus is one or more commodity markets (energy, metals, agriculture) and you need to track market-moving information alongside structured price and flow data.

## Relationship to base types

The base entity/concept/source types still apply:
- **Entities** — exchanges (e.g., `cme.md`), regulators, major producers
- **Concepts** — contango, backwardation, basis risk, seasonality
- **Sources** — EIA reports, S&P Global Platts, exchange publications
