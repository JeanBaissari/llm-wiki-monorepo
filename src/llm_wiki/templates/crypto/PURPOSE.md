# Domain: Crypto

The crypto domain tracks blockchain protocols, tokens, DeFi projects, and regulatory developments. It provides structured reference for the rapidly evolving crypto landscape — covering technical fundamentals, market dynamics, and the regulatory environment.

## When to use this domain

- Documenting blockchain protocol architecture and consensus mechanisms
- Tracking tokenomics, distribution schedules, and market data
- Cataloguing DeFi protocols — lending, DEXes, yield, derivatives
- Following regulatory frameworks, rulings, and compliance requirements
- Comparing chains, bridges, and interoperability solutions
