# Personal Growth — Domain Purpose

This template governs wikis for personal development tracking and reflective practice. It is designed for individuals committed to structured self-improvement, habit formation, and goal management.

## What this domain covers

- **Goals** — short-term and long-term objectives with target dates and progress tracking
- **Habits** — daily/weekly routines with frequency counts and streak tracking
- **Reflections** — periodic self-assessment, lessons learned, mindset shifts
- **Journal entries** — chronological records of thoughts, events, and insights

## When to use this template

Use when your wiki is a personal knowledge management system focused on self-improvement, accountability, and reflective writing.

## Relationship to base types

The base entity/concept/source types still apply:
- **Entities** — people (mentors, authors), books, courses, apps
- **Concepts** — metacognition, habit stacking, flow state, deliberate practice
- **Sources** — self-help books, research papers, podcasts, courses

## Naming conventions

- Goals: `goal-<verb>-<object>.md` (e.g., `goal-read-50-books.md`)
- Habits: `habit-<behavior>.md` (e.g., `habit-morning-meditation.md`)
- Reflections: `reflection-YYYY-MM.md` (e.g., `reflection-2025-06.md`)
- Journal: `journal-YYYY-MM-DD.md`
