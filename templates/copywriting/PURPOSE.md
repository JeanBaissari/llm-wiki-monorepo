# Domain: Copywriting

The copywriting domain captures written marketing and communication materials — ad copy, landing pages, email sequences, brand voice frameworks, and audience personas. It serves as a living reference for messaging strategy, tone, and creative execution across campaigns.

## When to use this domain

- Storing examples of effective copy for reference and reuse
- Documenting brand voice and tone frameworks
- Building audience personas with psychographic detail
- Managing campaign-specific copy assets and variations
- Tracking A/B test results and copy performance
