# Domain: Marketing

The marketing domain tracks go-to-market strategy, channel execution, campaign performance, competitive intelligence, and analytics. It provides structured insight into what channels drive results, how campaigns perform over time, and how competitors position themselves.

## When to use this domain

- Documenting channel strategy and performance benchmarks
- Tracking campaign lifecycle — planning, execution, analysis
- Centralizing competitive research and positioning analysis
- Recording analytics frameworks, dashboards, and key metrics
- A/B test results and optimization recommendations
