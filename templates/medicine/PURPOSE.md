# Medicine — Domain Purpose

This template governs wikis for structured medical knowledge management. It is designed for clinicians, medical researchers, students, and health-tech professionals who need a precise, evidence-based knowledge base of conditions, treatments, studies, and terminology.

## What this domain covers

- **Medical conditions** — etiology, pathophysiology, diagnostics, guidelines
- **Treatments** — pharmacological, surgical, behavioral, emerging therapies
- **Clinical studies** — structured trial data: design, sample size, outcomes, significance
- **Medical terminology** — precise definitions of terms, abbreviations, and classifications

## When to use this template

Use when your wiki's primary focus is clinical or biomedical knowledge, and you need rigorous tracking of evidence quality (study design, confidence, contradictions).

## Relationship to base types

The base entity/concept/source types still apply:
- **Entities** — drug manufacturers, medical device companies, research institutions
- **Concepts** — pathophysiology mechanisms, diagnostic criteria, staging systems
- **Sources** — journal articles, clinical practice guidelines, FDA documents

### Evidence quality

Every study page captures design, sample size, population, and statistical significance. The confidence and contested fields from the base schema should be used to flag contradictory results across studies.
