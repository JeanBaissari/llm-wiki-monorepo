# Reading — Domain Purpose

This template governs wikis for literary analysis and narrative study. It is designed for readers, book clubs, students of literature, and writers who need a structured knowledge base for tracking characters, themes, plot development, and chapter-level notes across works of fiction.

## What this domain covers

- **Characters** — profiles, arcs, relationships, character traits
- **Themes** — major ideas, motifs, symbolic analysis across the work
- **Plot threads** — narrative arcs, subplots, conflict and resolution tracking
- **Chapter notes** — chapter-by-chapter summaries, observations, questions

## When to use this template

Use when your wiki focuses on deep engagement with a single literary work or a set of related works, requiring structured character and plot tracking.

## Relationship to base types

The base entity/concept/source types still apply:
- **Entities** — authors, publishers, translators, illustrators
- **Concepts** — literary devices, narrative structures, genres
- **Sources** — literary criticism essays, interviews, annotated editions
