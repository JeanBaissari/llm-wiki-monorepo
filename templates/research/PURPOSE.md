# Research Wiki — Purpose

This template scaffolds a wiki for tracking and synthesising academic or
industry research on a specific topic.

**What it is for:**

- Building a living literature review with structured notes on papers,
  theses, methods, and findings.
- Organising research by methodology and thesis so claims and
  counter-claims can be traced, compared, and synthesised.
- Maintaining a durable personal or team knowledge base that grows with
  the research programme.

**What it is NOT for:**

- LaTeX manuscript writing or paper production — use a dedicated
  tool (Overleaf, Typst) for that.
- A code repository — use the `codebase` template instead.
- General project management or task tracking.

**Key extensibility pattern:** Add new methodology pages as you
encounter new techniques. Add cross-links between findings and the
thesis they support or challenge.
